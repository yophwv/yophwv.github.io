Amy Doh
PORTFOLIO ABOUT CONTACT

My name is Amy Doh and I am a digital illustrator from Korea with a focus on story-driven, scientific-based digital illustrations.

I was raised by scientists who kept me inspired by the wonders and advancements of science. This led me to have a great desire to illustrate complex scientific theories such that non-experts and academics alike can approach and appreciate my work in their own way.

Since then, my interests expanded to book illustrations, capturing nuances and positive emotion to visually-inspire readers.

My art delivers a unique experience to the audience, derived from heavy-research and utilization of complex emotions, such as melancholy and inquisitiveness, to express my thoughts on the world.

Currently, I am finishing a passion project at OCAD which utilizes the scientific theories around the laws of entropy to understand the concept of death in a new way. See you at GradX2024!

I always love a new creative story to work with! To contact me, visit my instagram, email me at yophwv@gmail.com, or contact me through the website.
